The first thing to creating simple website is a header. The header, being the most simple part of a website, shows anyone who comes to your website, what to expect from it. Also, all content that you write in html goes inside the body (<body></body>) tag.

This is my header from the first page that shows what my website is about. I am telling the viewer how to create a website.

          
            <body>
              <header>
                <h1>Creating a Website!</h1>
              </header>
            </body>

In this piece of html we have the header tag which tells the computer that this part is the header. Inside the header we have the header one tag or h1. When writing headers(h1), they go in order from one to six. Since we are in the header, we want to use the h1 tag to show this part is the most important.
